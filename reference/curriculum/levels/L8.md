# L8 · The River — "Who pays what's left?"

Patient responsibility. The bill flows downstream. The most
expansive level after the gap absorption — and the heaviest
emotionally.

Lean Hospital-side weight on patient stories; lean Waiting Room
surrealism for the release valve. The horror is *witnessed* in
the Hospital; the catharsis is *played* in the Waiting Room.
See [`../design-principles.md`](../design-principles.md).

## Concepts

- Patient cost-share waterfall (deductible → coinsurance →
  copay → OOP max).
- HDHPs and the deductible-cliff trap.
- **NSA (No Surprises Act).** (Gap: surprise billing,
  air-ambulance / out-of-network ER carve-outs, IDR process.)
- **Good-faith estimates (GFE).** (Gap.)
- Balance billing rules.
- Statement cadence.
- **Bad debt vs charity care vs financial assistance.** (Gap:
  the *positive* path — screen for charity, not collect.)
- **Payment plans.** (Gap.)
- AR aging from the patient side (linked to L7).

## Documents

- EOB.
- GFE.
- Patient statement.
- Financial assistance application.
- AR aging report (patient side).
- NSA disclosure forms.

## Actors

- Patient financial services (PFS) rep.
- **The patient as named NPC.** (Per design principle —
  patients can speak in Hospital rooms. Probably a small
  named-patient pool of 4–6 who recur; see
  [`../open-questions.md`](../open-questions.md), question 3.)
- State regulator (offstage in NSA cases).
- **Financial assistance counselor.** (Gap NPC.)

## Hospital rooms

- PFS counter.
- Billing window.
- **Financial assistance office.** (Gap.)
- Statement room (where the printed bills get processed).
- Counseling room for hard conversations.

## Waiting Room stations

- River of unread bills (general atmosphere).
- Ghost statements drifting on the surface.
- **Surprise Bill Specter** *(gap)* — appears in the ER wing
  wearing an out-of-network mask; polite, formal, devastating.
  Cosmological resonance: every NSA-violated balance bill.
- **Charity Lighthouse** *(gap)* — release-valve station that
  lights the way out. Players who screen for charity instead
  of collecting reach the lighthouse.

## Existing content

- `pr_204`, `co_109` — both real but neither carries the

  most of its content authored from scratch.**

## Gaps (most of the level)

- An NSA / surprise bill scenario (a patient hit with an
  out-of-network ER bill).
- A GFE scenario (a patient who got an estimate that was
  wildly wrong; how do we make it right?).
- A charity-care screening scenario (a patient who qualifies
  but doesn't know they do; PFS counselor's choice).
- A payment-plan scenario (a patient who can pay, but
  monthly).
- Surprise Bill Specter encounter.
- Charity Lighthouse station — non-combat, restorative.
- The named-patient pool (recurring patients who first
  appear at L2 and land here).

## Cross-cutting concerns introduced

- **Patient experience** (full weight here; touches L2, L3,
  L7 retroactively).
- **Health equity / financial harm** (the un-cheesy version
  of "the system is broken" — specific patients, specific
  consequences).
- **AR aging from the patient side** (touches L7).
