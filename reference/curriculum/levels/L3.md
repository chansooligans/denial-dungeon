# L3 · The Gate — "Should this service even happen yet?"

Prior authorization. The payer says: prove it first.

## Concepts

- Prior authorization (278 transaction).
- Notification vs auth vs predetermination — three different
  things that get conflated.
- Concurrent review for inpatient stays.
- Peer-to-peer review.
- Retroactive auth windows.

## Documents

- 278 request / response.
- Payer's UM criteria (e.g. MCG, InterQual).
- Peer-to-peer call notes.
