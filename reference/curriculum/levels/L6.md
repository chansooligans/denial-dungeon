## Narrative beats

Dana's L6 reveal beat is **the first wet ink**:

- A page that was blank yesterday has writing on it today.
  The ink hasn't dried.
- The player can't catch her writing it. They never see her
  with the notebook open.
- Page 8 (drafted) is the first page where Dana signs her
  name (`— Dana`) — she's never signed before. Subtle
  wrongness; she's worried this might be the last one she
  gets to write.
