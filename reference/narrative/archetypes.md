# Archetypes — cosmological resonance mapping

Every existing Waiting Room archetype is legible as a *kind of
unresolved-ness*. This file is the lookup table.

The cosmology rule (see [`cosmology.md`](cosmology.md)):
unresolved claims accumulate, and at scale their resonance
becomes manifest as creatures of the Waiting Room. Each
archetype names one specific failure pattern.

## Authoring rule

When inventing a new archetype, ask:

1. **What specific failure does this name?** Not "the system
   is broken" — *which CARC, which transaction, which form
   field*?
2. **What does the body look like?** The form-truth made
   physical. The Reaper has an hourglass-blade because
   timeliness is literally a blade. The Wraith is half-
   finished documentation because that's literally what she
   is.
3. **What resolves it?** The specific revenue-cycle action
   that makes the failure stop happening. In the cosmology,
   *that* action releases the resonance.

The mechanic of the archetype follows from #3. The teaching
of the archetype follows from #1. The vibe follows from #2.
