## 6. The player's annotation voice

When the player annotates Dana's notebook late game — what
register? Same wise-and-warm? Or distinctly the player's own
voice (whatever that is)?

Argument for *same voice*: the player has been steeping in
Dana's wisdom; their annotations reading like hers is *the
transformation made literal*.

Argument for *distinct voice*: the player isn't Dana; they're
their own analyst. Their voice should be earned over the run,
not inherited.

Probably distinct, but Dana-adjacent. To be drafted when we
write the player's notebook content.
